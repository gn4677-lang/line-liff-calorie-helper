# OKmart

## Role in v1

- Archetype-first convenience-store chain card.

## Safe Patterns

- Use generic convenience-store staples first: tea egg, sweet potato, chicken breast, salad, plain sandwich.
- Prefer unsweetened drinks and simple hot foods over snack stacking.
- Use current item names only when a live lookup confirms them.

## Risk Cues

- Weak official nutrition depth compared with FamilyMart.
- Hot snacks and sweet drinks.
- Assuming current promotional items are durable.
