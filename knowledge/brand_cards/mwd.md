# 麥味登 MWD

## Role in v1

- Breakfast-chain brand card with menu-family guidance and lower-risk ordering patterns.

## Safe Patterns

- Egg-based wraps, toast, or pancakes with black coffee or unsweetened tea.
- Clarify whether the user ordered a combo set or only the main item.
- Treat sweet drinks and side add-ons as separate calorie lanes.

## Risk Cues

- Large brunch sets.
- Sweet drinks plus bread or croffle stacking.
- Assuming a breakfast combo is equivalent to a single sandwich.
