# Q Burger

## Role in v1

- Breakfast-chain card with official menu-image, drink-label, allergy, and SGS surfaces.

## Safe Patterns

- Lighter egg, toast, or wrap items with black coffee or low-sugar drinks.
- Use category guidance first when exact menu-image extraction is unavailable.
- Confirm drink sweetness separately from the sandwich or wrap.

## Risk Cues

- Croffles, sweet drinks, and noodle or pasta combinations.
- Combo meals that quietly stack drink plus side.
- Treating image-only menu labels as precise without verification.
