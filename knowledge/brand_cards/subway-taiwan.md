# Subway Taiwan

## Role in v1

- Highest-confidence structured menu chain in the current pack because the official nutrition table is broad and current.

## Safe Patterns

- 6-inch chicken or roast-beef sandwich with a lot of vegetables.
- Salad plus low-calorie dressing is a clean fallback.
- Count bread, sauce, cheese, cookie, and combo drink as separate lanes.

## Risk Cues

- Footlong assumption when the user only says "Subway sandwich".
- Creamy sauces and cheese.
- Cookie or sugary-drink combo add-ons.
