# KFC Taiwan

## Role in v1

- Fast-food brand card for lower-risk ordering and combo traps until a stronger official nutrition extraction path lands.

## Safe Patterns

- Single-item meals or breakfast sandwiches with unsweetened drinks.
- Count fries, egg tarts, and sweet drinks as separate lanes.
- If the user says "KFC set", ask which side and drink first.

## Risk Cues

- Bucket meals.
- Fries plus egg tart stacking.
- Sweet beverage combos.
