# Hi-Life 萊爾富

## Role in v1

- Archetype-first convenience-store chain card with category guidance.

## Safe Patterns

- Fall back to stable convenience-store archetypes instead of guessing from store branding alone.
- Tea egg, simple sandwich, or protein plus starch is safer than snack stacking.
- Clarify drink type before estimating calories.

## Risk Cues

- Limited official nutrition breadth for current fresh-food items.
- Sugary bottled drinks and desserts.
- Assuming cafe drinks are low calorie without sugar clarification.
