# 可不可熟成紅茶 KEBUKE

## Role in v1

- Strong permanent drink chain because the official menu publishes medium `500 ml`, large `700 ml`, and many item calories.

## Safe Patterns

- 熟成紅茶 or 春芽冷露 with no sugar.
- Confirm medium or large before using any milk-tea calories.
- White pearls, cool-formula add-ons, and ice-cream drinks should each raise caution.

## Risk Cues

- 白玉奶茶 and other milk teas.
- Large cup sweet drinks.
- Ice-cream or cool-formula items with minimum ice rules.
