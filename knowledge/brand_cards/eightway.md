# 八方雲集

## Role in v1

- Dumpling-chain card for safe order templates when exact nutrition is not available.

## Safe Patterns

- Boiled dumplings or soup-centered orders usually beat pan-fried plus side-dish stacks.
- Count sauces and extra starch separately.
- Dumpling count matters more than store branding alone.

## Risk Cues

- Pan-fried dumplings plus soup plus side dish.
- Sauce-heavy orders.
- Assuming ten dumplings and six dumplings are interchangeable.
