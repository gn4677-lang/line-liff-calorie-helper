# 迷客夏 Milksha

## Role in v1

- Permanent chain card for taxonomy, toppings, and safe-order guidance.

## Safe Patterns

- Tea base or fresh-milk tea without toppings is the cleanest baseline.
- Clarify whether the drink is a pure tea, milk tea, or fresh-milk series.
- Keep pearls, grass jelly, and red bean as separate calorie lanes.

## Risk Cues

- Fresh milk drinks with toppings.
- Large sweet drinks.
- Assuming all Milksha drinks have public official calories.
