# FamilyMart 全家

## Role in v1

- Strongest Taiwan convenience-store chain for official fresh-food item cards and category coverage.

## Safe Patterns

- Sweet potato plus chicken breast or tea egg is a reliable low-friction meal.
- Salad cups work only if dressing is counted separately.
- Unsweetened tea, black coffee, and fruit boxes are useful low-risk lanes.

## Risk Cues

- Dessert plus sweet drink stacking.
- Creamy pasta, curry, or fried bentos.
- Treating volatile current SKUs as permanent truth beyond their observed date.
