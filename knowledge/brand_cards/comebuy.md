# COMEBUY

## Role in v1

- Taiwan drink chain with official menu, nutrition, and store locator coverage.

## Safe Patterns

- Pure tea with no sugar and no topping is the default low-risk lane.
- Ask size, sugar, ice, and toppings before estimating milk tea calories.
- Treat pearls and cream foam as separate calorie add-ons.

## Risk Cues

- Milk tea plus pearls.
- Large cup sweet drinks.
- Layered toppings or cream-based limited drinks.
